In today's fast paced world, change is inevitable. Driven by geopolitical turmoils and technological advances, like any other domain, the academic environment is subject to deviation, pushing us to adapt to the continuously changing conditions. Be it either the tools we work with, the subject we work on or for whom (i.e. public or private sector) we work, often we are faced with taking decisions related to our career. In this interactive session, we will briefly discuss our personal experiences on perceiving and managing the decision-making process.
